Future Career

Being a pilot for me is a chance to see the world from above, something some of us will never be able to experience. It makes you realize how small we are compared to this humongous planet, which I should say is one of the smallest in our solar system. Hopefully someday I’ll be able to say, “ welcome aboard this is captain Sal.”
